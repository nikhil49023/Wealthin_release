- For critical decisions, consult a financial advisor
- Wealthin is NOT liable for losses from AI recommendations

## FAQ

**Q: Can I use Wealthin offline?**  
A: Yes! All features work offline except AI. You can enter transactions, view reports, and manage budgets without internet.

**Q: Is my data backed up?**  
A: Locally on your device only, unless you enable Firebase backup (optional). You can export anytime.

**Q: Can I delete my data?**  
A: Yes. Delete individual items anytime, or full account deletion in Settings → Account → Delete All Data.

**Q: What happens when my free API limit resets?**  
A: Limit resets daily at 12:00 AM UTC.

**Q: Can I share my account?**  
A: No. Each person needs their own account for security and privacy.

**Q: How do I report bugs?**  
A: Email bugs@wealthin.app with screenshots and steps to reproduce.
